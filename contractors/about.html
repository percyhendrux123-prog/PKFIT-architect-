OUR STORY

Bakare's Contractors LLC was founded with a simple idea: do honest work at an honest price. What started as one person with a truck and a reputation for showing up on time has grown into a full-service contracting and landscaping company trusted across Baker and the greater Baton Rouge area.

We've always believed that the best advertising is a finished job that speaks for itself. That's why we focus on doing every project right the first time — no shortcuts, no excuses, no hidden costs. Our clients come back because they know what to expect: quality work, clear communication, and a team that treats their property like it's our own.

From remodeling kitchens and replacing roofs to maintaining lawns and clearing storm damage, we bring the same level of care and professionalism to every job — big or small.
