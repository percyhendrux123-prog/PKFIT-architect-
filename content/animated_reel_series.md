# PKFIT Animated Reels Series

This document defines a repeatable animated Reel format for PKFIT content.

## Series concept

- Series name: `SYSTEM > MOTIVATION`
- Format: 9:16 vertical (`1080x1920`), 30 to 45 seconds
- Style: dark background (`#080808`), gold accents (`#C8A96E`), kinetic typography, simple line graphics
- Voice: direct, calm, system-focused
- End card CTA: `Get The Blueprint` + `percyhendrux.gumroad.com/l/khcus`

## Episode template (copy/paste)

Use this structure for every Reel to keep production consistent.

1. Hook (0 to 3s)
   - One short contrarian statement.
2. Tension (3 to 10s)
   - Name the common mistake.
3. System shift (10 to 22s)
   - Introduce PKFIT principle in one sentence.
4. Action steps (22 to 35s)
   - Give three on-screen steps.
5. Close + CTA (35 to 45s)
   - Reinforce identity and direct to blueprint.
